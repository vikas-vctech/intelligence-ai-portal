Al Malaky Royal is an Ajman-based ultra-premium honey brand founded in 1972. Operating exclusively online via its own website and Amazon UAE, the brand positions itself as a supplier of 100% natural, lab-tested, and unadulterated honey sourced from Emirati bees and international apiaries. Its product range spans natural honey varieties, innovative honey spoon formats, bee products, and honeycombs — all certified HACCP, ISO 22000, and GMP. The brand's D2C model enables direct storytelling around traceability and purity.
