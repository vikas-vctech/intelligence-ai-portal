A methodology
you can cite

Every model runs a four-step waterfall — population architecture to scenario assessment. All assumptions carry named sources.

01
Population Architecture
Segments by nationality, income, and household composition — anchored to Gulf Statistical Centre census data.
02
Supply-Side Calibration
UN FAO production + UN Comtrade import flows triangulated against Euromonitor retail estimates.
03
Channel Decomposition
Retail household, HoReCa, food manufacturing, and gifting channels sized independently per country.
04
Scenario Assessment
Conservative, moderate, and aggressive growth paths with sub-category premiumisation curves to 2030.
